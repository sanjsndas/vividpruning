Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plants do you service?

Our team is experienced with a wide variety of trees, shrubs, hedges, flowers, and vegetable gardens. We adapt our pruning and irrigation techniques to suit each plant’s unique needs.

How often should pruning be done?

Typically, pruning is recommended once or twice a year depending on the species, growth rate, and health condition. Our experts can design a customized schedule for optimal results.

In-Depth Service Descriptions

Tree Pruning: Selective removal of branches to enhance tree structure, prevent hazards, and stimulate new growth. We carefully consider tree species and health to maximize longevity and safety.

Shrub & Hedge Trimming: Styling shrubs and hedges to maintain shape and vigor, including shaping topiaries and managing dense growth to prevent overcrowding and disease.

Irrigation System Installation: Designing drip, sprinkler, or smart systems tailored to your landscape size and plant water requirements. Installation includes piping, control systems, and programming for efficiency.

Irrigation System Maintenance: Periodic assessments to identify leaks, clogged emitters, or malfunctioning controllers, ensuring uninterrupted watering and system longevity.

Winter Pruning: Careful pruning done during dormancy to prepare plants for active growth in spring. Removing damaged wood to enhance overall health after winter stress.

Drip Irrigation Systems: Precise water delivery systems designed to minimize evaporation and runoff, ideal for gardens that require sustained moisture with minimal water waste.
